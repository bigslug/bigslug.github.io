(9/28/22)
out now on tapes scree: "am", an album by guests, my main music project.
marking a departure from the debut guests offering, "legacy, out back" which shunned all amplification in favor of exploring the power of the human voice,
this album embraces the electric whole-heartedly as guitars squeal and distortion washes over the mix like a wave breaking.
interspersed are momentary callbacks to guests earlier days -- folky interludes painting a picture of our narrator alone in nature,
but that picture rips and tears dropping the listener through a jagged opening to god-knows-where.
